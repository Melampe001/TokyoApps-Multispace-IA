## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around logos equal to the height of the "T" in TokyoApps®.

### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 120px width minimum
- **Print:** 1 inch width minimum

### Don'ts
- ❌ Do not stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Do not change logo colors
- ❌ Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, etc.)
- ❌ Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

## File Formats

### Vector
- **SVG:** Web and scalable use
- **EPS:** Print and professional design

### Raster
- **PNG:** Digital use with transparency
- **JPG:** Web use without transparency
- **Resolutions:** 1x, 2x, 3x for responsive design
